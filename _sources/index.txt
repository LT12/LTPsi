Welcome to LTPsi's documentation!
=================================
Restricted Hartree-Fock Quantum Chemistry Suite

Page in construction currently

Requirements:

- Python 2.7

- Numpy 1.6.0 or better

- Scipy 1.0.0 or better

- Numba

- C compiler (GCC or mingw32)

Optional Requirements for Additional Modules:

- Cython

- Ipython

- Matplotlib

- Mayavai2

- VPython

Additionally, it is recommended that Numpy/Scipy are linked to
either OpenBLAS, Intel MKL, ACML, or ATLAS
